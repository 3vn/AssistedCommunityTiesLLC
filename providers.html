Andy

Originally from Spokane, Washington, I moved to the Portland area in September 2015. During my free time I enjoy hiking, camping, rock climbing, skateboarding, and playing music. My favorite part about working with ACT is the friendships, the laughter, and the memories we all share together. Being a part of this amazing group of people has left a huge impact on my heart, and I am immensely grateful for this opportunity. I'm excited and look forward to an awesome future with Assisted Community Ties!
